# Email Service Alternatives for Tomodachi Tours

## Quick Setup Options

### 1. Resend (Recommended - Easy Setup)
- **Free tier**: 3,000 emails/month
- **Pricing**: $20/month for 50,000 emails
- **Setup time**: 5 minutes
- **Pros**: Modern, reliable, great developer experience

### 2. Mailgun
- **Free tier**: 5,000 emails/month for 3 months
- **Pricing**: $35/month for 50,000 emails
- **Setup time**: 10 minutes
- **Pros**: Reliable, good deliverability

### 3. Amazon SES
- **Pricing**: $0.10 per 1,000 emails
- **Setup time**: 15 minutes
- **Pros**: Very cheap, highly scalable
- **Cons**: Requires AWS setup

### 4. Postmark
- **Free tier**: 100 emails/month
- **Pricing**: $15/month for 10,000 emails
- **Setup time**: 5 minutes
- **Pros**: Excellent deliverability, focused on transactional emails
