# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification outlines the implementation of Local & Tourism SEO optimization for Tomodachi Tours website. This phase focuses on improving visibility in local search results, optimizing for tourism-specific keywords, and implementing location-based content strategies to attract travelers searching for Kyoto tour experiences.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Google Business Profile Integration

**User Story:** As a potential customer searching for "Kyoto tours near me", I want to see Tomodachi Tours in local search results and Google Maps, so that I can easily find and contact the business.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user searches for "Kyoto tours" or "English tours Kyoto" THEN Tomodachi Tours SHALL appear in Google's local pack results
2. WHEN the Google Business Profile is optimized THEN it SHALL include complete business information, photos, and customer reviews
3. WHEN local citations are implemented THEN business information SHALL be consistent across all directories (NAP consistency)
4. WHEN Google My Business is integrated THEN it SHALL sync with website structured data for consistent information

### Requirement 2: Location-Based Content Strategy

**User Story:** As a traveler planning a Kyoto visit, I want to find detailed information about specific neighborhoods and attractions, so that I can plan my itinerary effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN location-based content is created THEN it SHALL target specific Kyoto districts (Gion, Arashiyama, Fushimi, Uji)
2. WHEN neighborhood guides are implemented THEN they SHALL include local attractions, transportation, and cultural information
3. WHEN location pages are optimized THEN they SHALL target long-tail keywords like "things to do in Gion district"
4. WHEN content is structured THEN it SHALL provide value beyond just tour promotion

### Requirement 3: Tourism-Specific Keyword Optimization

**User Story:** As a search engine user looking for Kyoto experiences, I want to find relevant tour options when searching for specific activities, so that I can discover tours that match my interests.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN keyword research is implemented THEN it SHALL target tourism-specific terms like "Kyoto walking tours", "English guide Kyoto", "avoid crowds Kyoto"
2. WHEN content is optimized THEN it SHALL naturally incorporate target keywords without keyword stuffing
3. WHEN long-tail keywords are targeted THEN they SHALL address specific user intents like "early morning Fushimi Inari tour"
4. WHEN seasonal keywords are implemented THEN they SHALL target time-specific searches like "Kyoto cherry blossom tours"

### Requirement 4: Local Citation Building

**User Story:** As a search engine crawling the web, I want to find consistent business information across multiple authoritative sources, so that I can verify and rank the business appropriately in local search results.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN local citations are created THEN business information SHALL be submitted to major travel and local directories
2. WHEN NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency is maintained THEN information SHALL be identical across all platforms
3. WHEN travel-specific directories are targeted THEN listings SHALL be created on TripAdvisor, Viator, GetYourGuide, and similar platforms
4. WHEN citation quality is ensured THEN only authoritative, relevant directories SHALL be used

### Requirement 5: Review and Rating Management

**User Story:** As a potential customer researching tours, I want to see authentic reviews and ratings from previous customers, so that I can make an informed booking decision.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN review management is implemented THEN it SHALL encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and TripAdvisor
2. WHEN review schema is optimized THEN it SHALL display star ratings in search results
3. WHEN review responses are managed THEN all customer reviews SHALL receive professional, helpful responses
4. WHEN review monitoring is active THEN new reviews SHALL be tracked and addressed promptly

### Requirement 6: Mobile and Voice Search Optimization

**User Story:** As a traveler using mobile search or voice assistants, I want to find tour information quickly and easily, so that I can make bookings while on the go.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mobile optimization is implemented THEN the website SHALL load quickly and function perfectly on mobile devices
2. WHEN voice search is optimized THEN content SHALL target conversational queries like "What are the best English tours in Kyoto?"
3. WHEN local mobile searches are targeted THEN the site SHALL appear for "tours near me" type queries
4. WHEN mobile user experience is optimized THEN booking process SHALL be streamlined for mobile users

### Requirement 7: Seasonal Content Strategy

**User Story:** As a traveler planning a seasonal visit to Kyoto, I want to find information about the best tours for specific times of year, so that I can experience Kyoto at its most beautiful.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN seasonal content is created THEN it SHALL target time-specific searches like "Kyoto autumn tours" or "cherry blossom season tours"
2. WHEN seasonal keywords are implemented THEN they SHALL be naturally integrated into existing tour descriptions
3. WHEN seasonal landing pages are created THEN they SHALL provide valuable information about visiting Kyoto during specific seasons
4. WHEN seasonal content is updated THEN it SHALL be refreshed annually to maintain relevance

### Requirement 8: Competitor Analysis and Positioning

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to understand how competitors are ranking for tourism keywords, so that I can identify opportunities to outrank them.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN competitor analysis is conducted THEN it SHALL identify top-ranking competitors for target keywords
2. WHEN competitive gaps are identified THEN content strategy SHALL address underserved search queries
3. WHEN positioning is optimized THEN unique value propositions SHALL be emphasized (early morning tours, crowd avoidance)
4. WHEN competitive monitoring is implemented THEN ranking changes SHALL be tracked over time

### Requirement 9: Local Link Building Strategy

**User Story:** As a search engine evaluating website authority, I want to see relevant, high-quality links from local and tourism-related websites, so that I can assess the business's credibility and local relevance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN local link building is implemented THEN it SHALL target Kyoto tourism websites, local blogs, and travel resources
2. WHEN partnership opportunities are pursued THEN they SHALL result in natural, relevant backlinks
3. WHEN content marketing is used for link building THEN it SHALL provide genuine value to linking websites
4. WHEN link quality is maintained THEN only authoritative, relevant websites SHALL be targeted

### Requirement 10: Performance Monitoring and Analytics

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to track the effectiveness of local SEO efforts, so that I can measure ROI and optimize strategies based on data.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN local SEO tracking is implemented THEN it SHALL monitor local search rankings for target keywords
2. WHEN Google My Business insights are tracked THEN they SHALL show search queries, actions, and engagement metrics
3. WHEN conversion tracking is set up THEN it SHALL measure bookings from local search traffic
4. WHEN reporting is automated THEN monthly reports SHALL show progress on local SEO KPIs

### Requirement 11: Multi-Language SEO Foundation

**User Story:** As an international traveler who may not speak English as a first language, I want to find tour information in a format that's accessible to me, so that I can understand and book tours confidently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN multi-language foundation is established THEN it SHALL prepare the site structure for future Japanese language support
2. WHEN hreflang tags are implemented THEN they SHALL be ready for multi-language expansion
3. WHEN cultural content considerations are planned THEN they SHALL respect both international and local perspectives
4. WHEN language-specific keywords are researched THEN they SHALL inform future content strategy

### Requirement 12: Technical SEO for Local Search

**User Story:** As a search engine crawler, I want to easily understand the business's location, service areas, and local relevance, so that I can properly index and rank the website for local searches.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN technical local SEO is implemented THEN schema markup SHALL include precise geographic coordinates and service areas
2. WHEN local page optimization is completed THEN title tags and meta descriptions SHALL include location-specific keywords
3. WHEN internal linking is optimized THEN it SHALL connect location-based content effectively
4. WHEN site speed is optimized THEN Core Web Vitals SHALL meet Google's standards for mobile users
